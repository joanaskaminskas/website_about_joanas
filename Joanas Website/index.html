JSC Salove Spedition Manager 2007 05 - 2007 08

JSC Salove is international transport company located in Vilnius. Company has 27 trailers with semi-trailers and also 11 road trains. Due to succesful work JSC Salove on 2007th brought warehouse which is 8000 m2. Having such spaciouse warehouse company can increase transported amounts and spedition activity.

As a spedition manager I was searching for new clients and hauliers. Also planing routes, searching for full and part loads for partners trucks. As well working with Italian terminals.

JSC Vytaro Transportas Transport Manager 2007 08 - 2008 11

JSC "Vytaro transportas" was established on the 15th of July, 1994. The business relationships have been established with the most prominent vehicle transportation and logistics companies in Europe. High standards and requirements of partners determined specialization in a very specific field, i.e. transportation of new vehicles with the specialized cartransporters and expedition. Our company has put major efforts on staff training, teaching and investments have been made in order to ensure the compliance to the high service quality and transport technology standards and requirements of those companies.

As a transport manager I was searching for new clients, planing routes, searching for brand new cars manufacturers who wish to transport it to clients. I was working with Germany, Poland, Ukraine, Belorussia and Russia. Also i had to prepare drivers to route, control and solve problems during route. After route calculate drivers earned wage, check fuel and make invoices for clients.

JSC Samarina Transport Manager 2008 12 - present

UAB „Samarina“ was founded in 1994. The main activity of the company is international cargo transportation within countries of European Union. UAB „Samarina“ is a full member of the Lithuanian National Road Carriers’ Association “Linava”. We strive to be a modern transport company and to focus on the quality of the service. Service is improved continuously by investing in the customer service system, human resources, and vehicles.

As a transport manager I was searching for new clients, planing routes, searching for brand new cars manufacturers who wish to transport it to clients. I was working with almost all EU countries. Also I had to prepare drivers to route, control and solve problems during route. After received transport documents I had to make invoices for clients. Also I had to decide which drivers to take to work.
